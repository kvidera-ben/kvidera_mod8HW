Why I Chose the University of Iowa

Ever since I can remember, I was a Hawkeyes fan, so coming to the University of Iowa was an easy choice. I became an Iowa fan out of spite for my brother, who chose to be a Cyclones fan. Additionally, my closest friend growing up was also a big Iowa fan, and we ended up living together for three years until he graduated last year. Initially, I entered the university as a Psychology major with plans to attend law school. However, I soon realized that the subject was boring to me and I had no interest in going to law school, and I transferred to the College of Business. After taking Foundations of Business Analytics, I realized this was the career I wanted to pursue.
